We believe that every person deserves opportunity.

Opportunity to learn and apply technology to improve their lives, their work and our environment.

Opportunity to grow and participate in the world economy, to thrive individually and work collectively for a better world. And there is no better way to create opportunity than to combine education with technology.

With this in mind, we are building 10ten - a tech education community - to help you start and develop your journey in technology, by connecting you to great people to learn, exchange, build things and improve others, while having a lot of fun along the way.

If you want to become or develop yourself as a software engineer, web developer or product guy/girl, who is able to build your own technology, get a job at the coolest companies in the world, and disseminate knowledge that is relevant to humanity, this is the perfect place for you.

If you are just curious about the beauty and power of technology, this the right place for you too.

Let's get going.
